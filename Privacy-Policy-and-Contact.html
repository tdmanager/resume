Privacy Policy

Welcome to our website. We highly value and commit to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy aims to explain to you how we process your personal information, and your rights in the personal information processing activities.

How we collect and use your personal information
Personal information refers to any information recorded electronically or otherwise that relates to an identified or identifiable natural person, excluding anonymized information.

When you visit our website, we do not actively collect any of your personal information, nor do we use cookies or similar technologies to track or analyze your behavior. We only collect some basic device and network information, such as device type, operating system type, browser type, IP address, etc., for ensuring the normal operation of the website and providing an optimized user experience. This information will not be used to identify or contact you.

If you send us any information requests, opinions, suggestions or inquiries through our website, we may ask you to provide some contact information, such as name, email, phone number, etc., for communicating and responding to you. This information will only be used for processing your requests and will not be used for other purposes.

How we disclose and protect your personal information
We will not provide your personal information to any third party unless:

• You have expressly agreed in advance;

• Required by law or legal process;

• To protect our or the public's legitimate rights and interests.

We take reasonable technical and management measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, modification or disclosure. However, please note that no security measure is completely reliable, so we cannot guarantee that your personal information is absolutely secure.

Your rights in the personal information processing activities
According to applicable laws and regulations, you may have the following rights:

• To inquire, access, correct or delete your personal information;

• To withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information;

• To object or restrict our processing of your personal information;

• To request us to transfer your personal information to a third party;

• To complain to the relevant regulatory authorities.

If you want to exercise these rights, or have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the following ways:

admin@tgn9.com

How this privacy policy is updated
We may update this privacy policy according to business development or legal changes and post the latest version on this website. Please check this website regularly to learn about the latest privacy policy.

How to contact us
